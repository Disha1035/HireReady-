Exam Guidelines & Structure

Please read the assessment instructions carefully before starting. The system simulates official company environments.

  • 15 Randomized Questions: The test comprises 15 questions selected at random (5 Easy, 5 Medium, 5 Hard) from a validated recruitment test bank.
  • Strict Time Limit: You have exactly 10 minutes (600 seconds). A countdown timer will show in the header. The test auto-submits on timeout.
  • Question Palette: Use the interactive grid on the right sidebar to jump between questions. Color indicators show answered, visited, and flagged questions.
  • Mark for Review: You can flag questions to review them later. You can submit when they are still flagged; they will be scored if answers are selected.
  • Marking Scheme: Each correct answer earns 1 mark. There is no negative marking.
